1. Human Resource Audit:

With changes in recent federal and state employment laws, now may be a good time to conduct an HR Audit. Your HR audit will give you an objective look at your policies and HR practices and identify areas that may be improved or streamlined.

Our Comprehensive HR Audit will typically cover the following areas:

  • Organizational Assessment - current company overview, growth and review of company objectives to ensure alignment with the HR strategy.
  • Employee Life Cycle – recruitment, job description review, hiring and offer process, background screening, on-boarding, performance review process, termination process review.
  • Personnel, benefit and I-9 file audit
  • Federal and State Employee posting compliance; workers compensation
  • Policies & employee handbook
  • Total Rewards - compensation, benefits & payroll

Hire Ventures is your Human Resource Partner in completing a comprehensive HR Audit.

Your HR Audit is typically accomplished with an on site visit and tour of the facility, documentation review and final write up that will give you a written plan for your short and long term HR Strategy.

Employee Handbook Creation or Revision An employee handbook is one of the most important communication tools between you and your employees. It can provide guidance to managers and set expectations for employees and can also keep you legally sound if written well and updated as required. We offer customized employee handbook creation as well as revision to your current employee handbook. We partner with you to learn your company’s values, culture, lingo and tenants and we weave those into your employee policies throughout the handbook. ﻿ Unsure if you need a Handbook Review? If it has been more than two years, it’s safe to say, yes! Time for a review and update! A few things to think about: Do you allow your employees to bring their own devices and use them on your network? If so, do you have a BYOD policy? What are your social media guidelines? Do your employees understand them? Do you have the required disclaimers or does your handbook create an implied employment contract? Do you have an anti-discrimination, non-harassment and retaliation policy? Have you updated your Military Leave policy, FMLA, Safety, Immigration compliance and EEO laws with the federal changes outlined in the past 18 months?
